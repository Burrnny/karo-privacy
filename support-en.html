Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Klaro Pro purchase?

Settings (gear icon, top right) → Klaro Pro → "Restore Purchases". Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy.

Reporting a wrong question

Tap the flag icon ⚐ at the top right during a quiz. Select the reason (wrong answer, unclear, typo, ambiguous options, other). Your report is anonymous.

Enable or disable notifications

Settings → "Daily Reminder". You can also adjust the reminder time from there.

Erase my progress

Settings → "Reset Progress" (red button at the bottom). This action is permanent.

My streak broke

If you had a freeze ❄️ available, it was applied automatically. You receive one free freeze every month.
